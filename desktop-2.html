In 1941, Astounding Science Fiction magazine published a short story by a

little-known writer named Isaac Asimov.

The story was called "Nightfall", and many years later it has long been recognized

as a classic, its author a legend. Now, the Gran Master of Science Fiction teams with Robert

Silverberg, one of the field's top award-winning authors, to explore and expand an apocalyptic tale that

is more spellbinding today than ever before -- Nightfall: The Novel.
